
# Cart Test Plan

**Goal:** Make sure the shopping cart works correctly.

**What we will test:**
- Add a product to cart
- Add multiple products
- Remove products from cart
- Check cart total is correct

**How to test:** Manual testing

**Who will test:** 1-2 testers

**Where to test:** Chrome and mobile

**Steps to follow:**
1. Open product page
2. Click “Add to Cart”
3. Open cart page
4. Check products and total price
5. Remove or update quantity
6. Make sure cart updates correctly

**Done when:**  
All cart actions work properly and totals are correct.
